About Harborline

We are an independent software company on a mission to build tools that are useful, polished, and professional.

Our Story

Harborline Interactive was founded by Mohammad Shakib, a BCA student and software developer with a deep passion for software engineering, mobile applications, and the future of digital products.

What started as a desire to build better tools has grown into a commitment to creating software that respects users' time, intelligence, and workflow. Every product we ship is designed with care, attention to detail, and a relentless focus on quality.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between what independent developers can create and what users expect from modern software — utility that is powerful, interfaces that are beautiful, and experiences that feel effortless.

Our Mission

To create useful, polished, and professional software that empowers people to work smarter, stay focused, and get more done — without the bloat, complexity, or noise.
